Mission

Let’s HPC is an open-access online platform to supplement conventional classroom oriented High Performance Computing (HPC) and Parallel & Distributed Computing (PDC) education. We aim to provide tools to the HPC community that would aid users in learning, evaluating, teaching and seeing the performance of parallel algorithms from a system’s viewpoint.

How It Started

The precursor of the tools provided by the Let's HPC platform were offline versions of the tools that were made and used for a lab evaluation for the CS301-High Performance Computing course offered at DA-IICT during August-November 2016. This sparked the idea for the tools and the platform that today constitute the Let's HPC project.
